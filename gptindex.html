The Egg Cover

For DP Coon Traps

Born on the line. Built to last.

Designed by trappers who run in every kind of weather. The Egg Cover shields bait and lure while letting scent do its job.

  • Keeps bait and lure dry in rain, snow, and freeze–thaw cycles
  • Screw‑off egg top for fast access to bait chamber
  • Internal chamber sized for a cotton ball with lure
  • Two 45‑degree vents for optimized scent diffusion
  • Durable build that stands up to Wisconsin weather
  • Purpose‑built fit for DP coon traps

The problem: Moisture ruins bait and washes away expensive lure, tanking your set’s performance.

The solution: The Egg Cover keeps your bait investment protected while venting scent to draw animals to your sets—no matter the forecast.

Our Story

The Family: Jason, Emily, Lexi, and Kathryn. Licensed trappers in Wisconsin, teaching the next generation the craft.

Daddy Daughter Trapping started with Emily’s first raccoon and grew into something bigger. Cold mornings on the line, hauling big beavers, and solving real landowner problems forged our approach: practical gear that works.

The Egg Cover came from a real need—keeping bait dry and lures effective when weather turns nasty.

This is our first product, designed by trappers who live this life every season.
